CHIPS WAYA WAYA

The Home Of Tasty

Chips Waya Waya is a popular fast-food business established in 2018, located in the heart of Sky City, Alberton. Founded by local entrepreneur Thabo Ndlovu, the business started as a single mobile cart known for its perfectly seasoned and crispy “Waya Waya” chips and the famous South African Kota.

Due to overwhelming community support and a reputation for generous portions, bold flavours, and excellent service, the business expanded into a permanent takeaway-focused storefront in 2022. Today, Chips Waya Waya continues to provide delicious meals and a true kasi food experience to customers every day.

Mission

To serve the Sky City community with the most authentic, flavourful, and generously portioned Kota meals, prepared with fresh ingredients and served with pride.

Vision

To become the most recognized and beloved fast-food brand in Alberton, expanding from a local gem into a multi-location business while maintaining the soul of its street-food origins.
